Correlation and Regression

The correlation between two variables measures the relationship between these two variabes. Correlation only measures linear relationships.

The measure of linear association is called the correlation coefficient and is denoted by r.

Properties of the correlation coefficient:

  • r is between 1 and -1
  • the sign indicates positive or negative slope
